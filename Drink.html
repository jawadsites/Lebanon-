My favourite things to drink in Lebanon are:

    • Ayran (Laban): A refreshing yogurt drink, sometimes enjoyed with meals.
    • Jallab: A sweet drink made from grape molasses and rose water, served with pine nuts and raisins.
    • Mint Lemonade (Lemonada): A refreshing blend of lemon juice, sugar, and fresh mint, especially popular in summer.
    • Coffee (Ahweh): Strong and aromatic, Lebanese coffee is similar to Turkish coffee, served in small cups and often flavored with cardamom.
